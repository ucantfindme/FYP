    Rules

    1. Dificuty modes: Easy, Medium, Hard.

    2. Types of Movies: Language specific, Country specific, Global.

    3. In each game the player will be given 10 question, each carrying 100 points.

    4. Each question has a time-limit based on dificulty mode.

    5. With in time answering, the extra time will be added to the points awarded.

    6. Every wrong answer will be awarded with a negative 50 points.

    7. Hints are provided.
      • First hint causes a negitive 10 to the total points rewarded, i.e, a total of 90(100-10) points will be given to the correct answer.

      • Second and third hint causes a negitive 20 to the total points rewarded, i.e, a total of 70(100-10-20) or 50(100-10-20-20) points will be given to the correct answer respectivly.

    8. Titles are given to a player based on final score.
      • Above 900 : Film buff/ Game master

      • 700-900 : Movie lover

      • 500-700 : Amateur

      • 200-500 : Noob

      • Below 200 : Go watch some movies

    9. For Team trivia prior to the game,
      • A host will be decided, who decides all the filters needed.

      • Team divisions are done randomly or the captains can choose.
